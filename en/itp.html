Frequently asked questions

What you should know about the ITP

What documents do I need for the ITP?

The registration certificate (talon) and valid RCA insurance. We also recommend the owner's ID.

How long does an inspection take?

On average 20–30 minutes for a passenger car, if the vehicle meets the technical conditions.

How long is the ITP valid?

For passenger cars, generally 2 years. For taxis, passenger transport or older vehicles, validity may be 1 year or 6 months.

What happens if the car doesn't pass?

You receive a report with the defects found. After fixing them you can return for a re-inspection, within the legal term.

Can I come without an appointment?

We recommend booking to avoid waiting times, but you can call us anytime at 0744 773 698 for availability.

Will you remind me when the ITP expires?

Yes. On request we remind you for free before expiry, so you don't end up with the inspection undone.
