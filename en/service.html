Core service

6D wheel alignment

We measure and precisely correct the wheel angles, so the car drives straight, tyres wear evenly and fuel consumption drops.

  • ✓ Caster
  • ✓ Camber
  • ✓ Toe (convergence / divergence)
  • ✓ Printed report with the before and after values

When do you need an alignment?

  • 🔸 The steering wheel isn't straight while driving
  • 🔸 The car "pulls" to one side
  • 🔸 Tyres worn unevenly
  • 🔸 After hitting kerbs / potholes
  • 🔸 After changing tyres or steering parts
